H.B. Reese, a former Hershey dairy farm employee, invented Reese's Peanut Butter Cups in his basement in 1928. The candy was originally called "Penny Cups" because they cost one cent. Reese used Hershey chocolate in his candies. The Hershey Company eventually bought Reese's business in 1963.
